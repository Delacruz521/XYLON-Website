Benifits of the website

Trees play a vital role in our ecosystem. Their significance to us is greater than anything that nothing and no one can ignore nor deny. As such, they are considered to be the lungs of the planet because through the process of photosynthesis they take carbon dioxide (CO2) from the air and water from the ground hence, in the process of converting it into wood, they release oxygen into the air. Even so, trees stabilize the soil, give life to the world's wildlife, and provide us with the materials for tools and shelter. However, the diminishing number of trees made upon the destructive actions of humans is widely concerning .

PARTNERSHIP

01. Forest Management Bureau (FMB)

These bureau's three programs are State Forest Nursery, Fox Research and Demonstration Forest, and Forest Management. The bureau is responsible for the production and marketing of seedlings for forestry and conservation, the administration of forests under state authority, research, demonstration, and the promotion of scientific forestry.

02. KADASIG MICHAELINIAN ORGANIZATION

This is a school-based organization in Saint Michael College of Caraga that seeks to influence students with resiliency, discipline, and responsibility through their programs such as their recent activity partnership with other organization, "The Mangrove tree planting" held at Brgy. Abilan Coastal Area, Buenavista.

03. Ecosystems Research and Development Bureau (ERDB)

The Department of Environment and Natural Resources' main unit for research and development (R & D). The five main ecosystems of the Philippines— forests, upland farms, grassland and degraded regions, coastal zone and freshwater, and urban areas—are the focus of its research and development and extension operations

04. Protected Areas and Wildlife Bureau (PAWB)

An agency of the Department of the Environment and Natural Resources of the Republic of the Philippines is in charge of protecting the country's biological variety. It envisions a network of protected areas and other important biological parts of the environment being managed by informed, capable individuals for the sustained use and enjoyment of current and future generations as a perpetual existence of biological and physical diversity.

05.Philippines’ National Greening Program (NGP)

In order to lessen flooding, landslides, and soil erosion, it seeks to hasten the rehabilitation and regrowth of landscapes.
